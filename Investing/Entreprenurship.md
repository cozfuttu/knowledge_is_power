### Who do we need in a startup
Mainly 2: 
one strategy person (ceo): Define vision & mission; Talk to users, investors, partners
one tech person (cto): the person who codes

Then 3:
Product manager: ux (user experience) person, maybe design skills
Marketing: social media & partnerships
Operations & finance: Handle money, runway, contracts; support fundraising
